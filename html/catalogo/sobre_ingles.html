About us


The Cervejaria Independente Vera Cruz S.A. has invaded our ports to revolutionize the market, rescuing the soul and flavor of the old beer recipes, bringing a line of special and differentiated beers.

Our mission is to produce super premium beers and draft beer, pure malt, using extremely selected raw materials. Our recipes are unique, carefully crafted by our Brewmaster and strictly follow the Bavarian Purity Law (Reinheitsgebot), dated 1516.

Based in Indaiatuba and selected distribution, the brewery has created a portfolio of unique and differentiated products, based on rare recipes that use precious ingredients imported from various regions of the world.

We seek to spread a high quality standard to lovers of good beer and to rescue values forgotten or never mentioned by the great industrial breweries.

Karavelle's proposal is to represent a moment of discovery for Brazilian consumers, a kind of Great Navigation in the fabulous world of special beers.

The biggest difference between the Karavelle beers is perceived in the first drink: a taste that comes like the winds that blow to sail on a unique palate and that transports us to a New World.
